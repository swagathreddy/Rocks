Rock Formation Challenge

Why do rocks look and feel so different from one another? Some have tiny bits that sparkle like glitter, while others are just one solid colour. Some feel coarse and gritty, while others are smooth to the touch. There are thousands of different rocks on Earth. As you know, scientists have grouped them into three main types: igneous, sedimentary, and metamorphic—based on how each one forms in nature.
